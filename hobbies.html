2018 Hackathon - Opal Iced Coffee Maker

The Opal Iced Coffee Maker came to life at the GE Appliances Hack the Home: Mega Hackathon hosted by FirstBuild in Louisville, KY. After drinking much iced coffee during a tough semester of classes and often paying several dollars at a coffee shop across campus I began to wonder why there wasn't an easy & instant way to make iced coffee at home. The new kitchen appliance was built in less than 24 hours from a FirstBuild Opal Nugget Ice Maker and a Mr. Coffee® coffee maker. It turns 200°F fresh brewed coffee into 55°F iced coffee which way cool enough that the coffee isn't watered down when ice is added. The Opal Iced Coffee Maker was recognized by US Steel for best use of their sheet metal and came in first for their sponsor challenge.
