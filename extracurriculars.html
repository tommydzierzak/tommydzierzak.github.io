Campus and Service Involvements

Toledo YES Project

Toledo YES (Youth elect service) Project is an annual service project that helps those in need residing in Toledo Ohio. It involves 140+ high-school age volunteers serving on roughly 12 different sites across Toledo. I have been involved for 8 years after participating as a high schooler and have gained responsibility eventually serving as as a co-director in charge of panning and executing.

International Co-op Program

Over the past semesters I have been studying German so that I can co-op abroad in Germany. These courses include an intensive 6-week course that took me from no prior knowledge of German to a German 3 level of knowledge. The following german course was designed to keep our German skills sharp as well as teach us about living in Germany and German culture. Spring & Summer 2019 semesters I hope put my German skills to the test while living and working abroad.

CEAS Ambassadors

CEAS Ambassadors is a service organization that works toward advancing the relationship between the students, alumni, faculty, staff and friends of the College of Engineering and Applied Science. We complete this task by welcoming visitors and sharing our college of engineering with them.

Triangle Fraternity

Greek engineering organization focused on academics and brotherhood. Our chapter has been recognized as the as the highest GPA greek organization on UC's campus. I have served as a new member chairperson.
